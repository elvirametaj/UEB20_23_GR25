Travel Packing List:
  1. Make a Travel Checklist:
    • Passport and visa
    • Travel itinerary and reservations
    • Important documents (insurance, identification)
    • Money (cash, credit cards)
  2. Clothing:
    • Underwear and socks
    • Shirts and pants
    • Jackets or sweaters
    • Pajamas
    • Swimwear
    • Comfortable walking shoes
  3. Toiletries:
    • Toothbrush and toothpaste
    • Shampoo and conditioner
    • Body wash or soap
    • Razor and shaving cream
    • Deodorant
    • Hairbrush or comb
